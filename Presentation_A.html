Context and Problem Statement

Product returns on Amazon represent a significant cost for many stores and impact customer satisfaction. Identifying high-risk products before they are listed is essential to optimize inventory and reduce losses.

The proposed AI solution provides a fast and efficient approach to predict the return risk of a product. The goal is to demonstrate the feasibility of AI-based prediction before a full-scale deployment.

The prototype typically includes:

  • An AI model capable of assessing the return risk for each product.
  • A visualization interface displaying products and their associated risk score.
  • A demonstration based on existing data (price, description, reviews, ASIN, images).

Importance of this project: Testing AI effectiveness before developing a complete solution helps reduce financial and technical risks, while providing actionable insights for product management.

This interface provides you with complete documentation of the proposed solution, a test module to evaluate any product available on Amazon, as well as a “Perspectives” section presenting the next steps for improving the model.
