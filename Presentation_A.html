Context and Problem Statement

Product returns on Amazon represent a significant cost for Ponera and affect customer satisfaction. Identifying high-risk products before they are listed is essential to optimize inventory and reduce losses.

The proposed MVP (Minimum Viable Product) serves as a fast and effective technical solution to predict the return risk of a product. The goal is to demonstrate the feasibility of AI-based prediction before a full deployment.

The MVP typically includes:

  • An AI model capable of evaluating the return risk for each product.
  • A visualization interface showing products and their associated risk scores.
  • A demonstration based on available data (price, description, reviews, ASIN, images).

Importance of the MVP: Testing the AI effectiveness before developing a complete solution reduces financial and technical risks while providing actionable insights for product management.

This interface provides you with comprehensive documentation of the proposed solution, a testing module to evaluate any product available on Amazon, and a “Perspectives” section outlining the next steps for improving the model.
